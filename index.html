Elounda Relax Apartments

Elounda Relax Apartments is a small resort of 7 spacious apartments situated in Elounda, 200m from the sea in a serene location at the top of the town. All the apartments have a cosy living room with a dining table and a sofa (convertible to a bed), fully equipped kitchen, bedroom, spacious bathroom, and free WIFI. They also have access to a patio or balcony with fantastic views. The bedrooms are equipped with either a double bed or 2 single beds and come with air-conditioning, big wardrobes, and safes. There are also parking spaces in a nearby area.

The apartments offer a cozy vacation spot, perfect for a calm and peaceful getaway. There is a majestic view of Elounda bay and Spinalonga. The town square of Elounda is 200m from our location, and has a variety of amenities including supermarkets, taverns, and many shops. There are nearby beaches and interesting sea-side locations such as the picturesque Venetian saltpans. Plaka, the beautiful village 5km away has traditional taverns and wonderful pebble beaches. You can also take a boat ride to the Spinalonga island from Plaka or Elounda. Agios Nikolaos is 10 km away, and the airport of Heraklion is 70 km away.
